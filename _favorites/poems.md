<h2>Ennui</h2>
<div class="stanza">
<span class="verse">He often expressed</span><br>
<span class="verse">A curious wish,</span><br>
<span class="verse">To be interchangeably</span><br>
<span class="verse">Man and fish;</span><br>

<span class="verse">Off the hook,</span><br>

<figure class="poem">
<h2>"I shall go back"</h2>
<div class="stanza">
<span class="verse">I shall go back again to the bleak shore</span><br>
<span class="verse">And build a little shanty on the sand,</span><br>
<span class="verse">In such a way that the extremest band</span><br>
<span class="verse">Of brittle seaweed will escape my door</span><br>
<span class="verse">But by a yard or two; and nevermore</span><br>
<span class="verse">Shall I return to take you by the hand;</span><br>
<span class="verse">I shall be gone to what I understand,</span><br>
<span class="verse">And happier than I ever was before.</span><br>
<span class="verse">The love that stood a moment in your eyes,</span><br>
<span class="verse">The words that lay a moment on your tongue,</span><br>
<span class="verse">Are one with all that in a moment dies,</span><br>
<span class="verse">A little under-said and over-sung.</span><br>
<span class="verse">But I shall find the sullen rocks and skies</span><br>
<span class="verse">Unchanged from what they were when I was young.</span><br>
</div><figcaption>Edna St. Vincent Millay</figcaption></figure>

<figure class="poem">
<h2>Mad Girl's Love Song</h2>
<div class="stanza">
<span class="verse">I shut my eyes and all the world drops dead;</span><br>
<span class="verse">I lift my lids and all is born again.</span><br>
<span class="verse">(I think I made you up inside my head.)</span><br>
</div><div class="stanza">
<span class="verse">The stars go waltzing out in blue and red,</span><br>
<span class="verse">And arbitrary blackness gallops in:</span><br>
<span class="verse">I shut my eyes and all the world drops dead.</span><br>
</div><div class="stanza">
<span class="verse">I dreamed that you bewitched me into bed</span><br>

<span class="verse">(I think I made you up inside my head.)</span><br>
</div><div class="stanza">
<span class="verse">God topples from the sky, hell's fires fade:</span><br>
<span class="verse">Exit seraphim and Satan's men:</span><br>
<span class="verse">I shut my eyes and all the world drops dead.</span><br>
</div><div class="stanza">
<span class="verse">I fancied you'd return the way you said,</span><br>
<span class="verse">But I grow old and I forget your name.</span><br>
<span class="verse">(I think I made you up inside my head.)</span><br>
</div><div class="stanza">
<span class="verse">I should have loved a thunderbird instead;</span><br>
<span class="verse">At least when spring comes they roar back again.</span><br>
<span class="verse">I shut my eyes and all the world drops dead.</span><br>
<span class="verse">(I think I made you up inside my head.)</span><br>
</div><figcaption>Sylvia Plath</figcaption></figure>

</div><figcaption>Francis William Bourdillon</figcaption></figure>
